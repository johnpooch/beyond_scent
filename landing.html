Beyond Scent

Since leaving school, I have explored a number of potential career paths but I never found a role which felt like a perfect fit for me.

One of the career paths which I explored was fragrance marketing. During this period I worked closely with designers and realised that design was my vocation.

This creative submission is inspired by my experience in the fragrance marketing industry and how it led me towards design. The submission takes the form of a mini perfume catalogue. The search for a signature perfume acts as a fitting metaphor for my search for the perfect career. Each fragrance represents a stage in my life which led me to finding the Shillington School of Graphic Design, my signature scent identity.
